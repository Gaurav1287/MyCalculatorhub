Best LIC Policies for Savings

1. LIC Jeevan Anand

This is one of the most popular LIC plans that combines savings and life insurance benefits. It offers maturity benefits and lifetime coverage.

2. LIC New Endowment Plan

This plan is ideal for those looking for long-term savings with life coverage. It provides guaranteed bonuses and maturity benefits.

3. LIC Jeevan Labh

A limited premium paying plan that offers high returns and protection to your family. Ideal for medium to long-term savings.

4. LIC Money Back 25 Years

This plan provides periodic payouts along with life insurance coverage. Great for those who need liquidity at regular intervals.
