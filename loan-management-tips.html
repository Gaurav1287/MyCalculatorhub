Loan Management Tips

1. Understand Your Loan Terms

Before taking a loan, understand the interest rate, tenure, and EMI structure. This helps in planning your finances better.

2. Pay More Than the Minimum EMI

If possible, pay extra towards your loan. This reduces the principal amount and saves you money on interest.

3. Refinance for Better Interest Rates

Keep an eye out for lower interest rates and consider refinancing your loan if it reduces your financial burden.

4. Automate EMI Payments

Set up automatic payments to avoid missing EMIs and incurring penalties.

5. Maintain a Good Credit Score

A good credit score helps you get better loan offers and lower interest rates. Pay your bills on time to maintain a healthy credit profile.
